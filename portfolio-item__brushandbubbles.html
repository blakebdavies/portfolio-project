Case Study: Brush & Bubbles

Shopify E-Commerce Website

Brush and Bubbles is a small art events company based in London, England. Launching in 2017, their mission was to bring step-by-step painting to the masses with a glass of bubbles to accompany the creativity. When the Covid-19 Pandemic, hit they were no longer able to run their regular classes, and struggled to connect with their customer base in order to stay afloat during the increasingly uncertain times.

As the Front-end Developer, I was responsible of building the entire UI for the new website, redefining the User Experience and studying new interactions between the User and the Interface. One of the most exciting experiences was integrating the entire front-end system with the Shopify Platform, which allowed a user-friendly sales experience from their primary social channel, Instagram.

This transition proved extremely successful, resulting in increased web traffic, product sales, and SEO opportunities to take this small local business to a globally in-demand arts company.
